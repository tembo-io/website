Find in **Settings > Instance Settings > Name**

![rename-instance](./rename-instance.png)

Click on the **Name** field and enter the new name for your instance.

Click on the **Save Changes** button to apply the new name.

When you rename your instance, it will show **Updating** in the Tembo Cloud UI. Once the instance is successfully updated, you will receive a notification confirming the update.
